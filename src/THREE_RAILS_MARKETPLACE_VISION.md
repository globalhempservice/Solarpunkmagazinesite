## ğŸ’° MONETIZATION SUMMARY

### **SWAP (C2C):**
- Shipping/logistics fees
- Trust verification ($5-10 one-time)
- Boosted listings (NADA)
- Swap insurance (optional)
- **Target:** $2-5 per completed swap

### **SWAG (B2C/B2B):**
- Traffic/lead referral fees
- SaaS subscriptions for orgs ($50-200/mo)
- Transaction fees (if internal checkout)
- Featured listings
- **Target:** $10-50 per month per org

### **RFP (B2B):**
- Match request fees (3 NADA = ~$3-5)
- Success fees (2-5% of deal value)
- Power user subscriptions ($100-500/mo)
- Priority matching
- **Target:** $100-5000 per closed deal

**Survival Math:**
- 100 swaps/month = $200-500
- 50 orgs on SWAG SaaS = $2,500-10,000
- 10 RFP deals/month = $1,000-50,000

**RFP is the engine. SWAP and SWAG are the community.**
